Our Mission

We're tackling plastic pollution by transforming waste into valuable resources

Collect

We gather plastic waste from our community, focusing on #5 polypropylene that would otherwise end up in landfills.

Process

Using open-source machines, we shred and transform plastic waste into reusable materials.

Create

We design and manufacture durable products that serve our community while reducing waste.
