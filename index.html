The Plastic Transformation Process

From waste to wonder in four revolutionary steps

1

Collection & Sorting

Community-sourced #5 polypropylene rigorously cleaned and prepared

2

Shredding

Plastic waste reduced to uniform flakes for consistent processing

3

Extrusion

Flakes melted and formed into durable beams or custom shapes

4

Fabrication

Transformed into functional products for campus and community
