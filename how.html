How It Works

We take #5 polypropylene plastic and transform it into durable, useful products through these steps:

  1. Collection: We gather clean #5 plastic cups, chairs, and containers from schools and local businesses.
  2. Shredding: Plastics are sorted by color and shredded into flakes using our in-house shredder.
  3. Melting: Flakes are heated in an extruder or injector depending on the product.
  4. Shaping: The molten plastic is injected into molds or extruded into beams.
  5. Finishing: Products are cooled, trimmed, tested for durability, and distributed or donated.

Our team documents every step to improve design, reduce waste, and share what we learn.
