Our Recycling Process

1

Collection

We gather #5 polypropylene (PP) plastic from campus and local community donations.

Accepted items: Yogurt containers, bottle caps, some disposable cups

2

Preparation

All plastic is thoroughly washed and sorted by color and type.

Quality control: Only clean, properly identified PP is processed

3

Shredding

Plastic is fed into our shredder, reducing it to uniform 8-10mm flakes.

Output: ~15kg/hour of processed flakes

4

Extrusion

Shredded plastic is melted and formed into beams or sheets using our extruder.

Temperature: 200-220Â°C depending on plastic type

5

Fabrication

Extruded material is cut, shaped, and assembled into final products.

Current products: Benches, chairs, and custom projects

Data Tracking

We document every step of our process to improve efficiency and share knowledge:

  • Plastic input by type and quantity
  • Machine settings and output
  • Product specifications
