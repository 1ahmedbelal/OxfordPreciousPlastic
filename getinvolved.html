Get Involved

Whether you're a student, teacher, business, or community member — there's a way to help.

  • Donate Plastics: We accept clean, #5 plastic items like cups, containers, and furniture.
  • Join the Team: Students can join our workspace and help with shredding, extrusion, and outreach.
  • Sponsor a Bench: Fund a bench for a local park or school and we’ll create it from recycled plastic.
  • Share Our Mission: Follow us on Instagram, spread the word, and invite others to reduce plastic waste.

Interested? Reach out to oxfordpreciousplastic@gmail.com or visit us in person.
