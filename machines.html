Machines We Use

Plastic Shredder

This machine cuts plastic into small flakes, preparing it for further processing. It's the first step in our recycling workflow.

Extruder

The extruder melts shredded plastic and pushes it through a mold to form beams, filament, or other shapes for use in construction or design.

Injection Molder

This machine compresses molten plastic into reusable molds, helping us produce strong, durable objects like coasters and tools.
