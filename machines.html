Operation Guides

Shredder Operation

  1. Sort plastic by type and color
  2. Check mesh installation
  3. Turn on machine
  4. Feed plastic gradually
  5. Store shredded plastic
  6. Clean after use

Extruder Operation

  1. Heat to 200-220Â°C
  2. Wait 20 minutes
  3. Fill hopper with plastic
  4. Turn on motor
  5. Discard first 2 minutes of output
  6. Collect extruded material
