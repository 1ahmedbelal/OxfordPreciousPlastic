About Us

Oxford High School Precious Plastic is Mississippi's first student-led workspace for recycling #5 plastic into long-lasting community products. We were founded to tackle the lack of local recycling for polypropylene, educate students on sustainable design, and give back to our city through innovation.

We collect waste from local businesses and schools, shred it, and transform it using extrusion and injection techniques into usable items like benches, chairs, and picture frames.
